ABOUT DES

I'm an animal and nature lover, I'm adventurous and I'm creative...

Having originally obtained a BSc. in Multimedia computing my career path developed into Data Analytics, working for some of the world's largest multinationals.

An adventurer by nature I recently took a career break and went travelling for a year, exploring the world and it's amazing countries and people. During this time away I decided that I wanted to change my career direction.

I found my new career goal while studying the fields of UI & UX Design. I have rediscovered my love for design and have greatly enjoyed the new journey that I am currently on.

In addition I have recently completed a Diploma in Software Development and have a working knowledge of front-end coding. I can use these skills to work with development teams or to build interactive prototypes.
