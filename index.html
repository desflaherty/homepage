ABOUT DES

Having originally obtained a BSc. in Multimedia computing my career path developed into Data Analytics, working for some of the world's largest multinationals.

An adventurer by nature I recently travelled for a year, exploring the world and it's amazing countries. During this time away I decided that I wanted to change my career direction.

I found my new career goal while studying the fields of UX & UI design and have been investing my time re-skilling in this area.

I enjoy using insights to design visually attractive products that solve problems and improve people's lives.

I also have a working knowledge of Front-end coding. I can use these skills to work with development teams or to build interactive prototypes.
