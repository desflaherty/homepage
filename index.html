ABOUT ME

I originally obtained a B.Sc. in Multimedia Computing but my career to date has been mainly in Data Analytics, working for some of the world's largest multinationals in a variety of industries. I have recently re-discovered my love for design however, and am making a career transition back into what I truly enjoy - a fresh start which I am excited and energised by!

I have developed a number of transferable skills working as an analyst that I can bring to a new UX role. I have experience analysing customer and product data and as UX is a discipline that involves understanding the needs and motivations of others this experience will add greatly to any UX project I am invloved in.

Data visualization was also an area of my former role that I enjoyed, developing aesthetically pleasing and informative dashboards. I will bring these creative skills into my new profession, and look forward to designing new Interfaces or re-designing existing products.

In addition I have recently completed a Diploma in Software Development and am proficient with front-end coding to include HTML5, CSS3, JS and Python. I can work with development teams on UX/UI projects as I can speak their language..

My portfolio section contains UX, UI, Web Development and Graphic Design projects that I have completed in various courses over the last year of this new journey.
