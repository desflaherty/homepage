ABOUT ME

Adventurous,Creative,Analytical,Determined.

Having originally obtained a BSc. in Multimedia Computing my career path developed into Data Analytics, working for some of the world's largest multinationals in a variety of industries. There was something missing though....I felt as if I was on the wrong path.... I missed the creativity that I remembered from my days in college...I felt unfulfilled in my chosen profession. An Adventurer by nature I took a break and went travelling for a year, exploring the world, it's amazing countries and people. Travel further awakened my creative side and when I returned to Dublin, I decided I needed a change...and finally I made my move.

During the past year I have been exploring other avenues to determine what career is right for me. I found my new career when I became aware of the field of User Experience and User Interface Design. While studying these disciplines I rediscovered my love for design and have greatly enjoyed the new journey that I am currently on.

With my new new skills I also bring experience..I am well versed in analysing customer data, and as UX is a discipline that involves understanding the needs and motivations of others this experience will greatly add to any UX projects that I am invloved in. Data visualization was also an area in my former role that I enjoyed and I will bring these creative skills into my new profession, and look forward to designing new Interfaces or re-designing existing products.

In addition I have recently completed a Diploma in Software Development and have a working knowledge of front-end coding. I can use these skills to work with development teams on any UX/UI projects.
