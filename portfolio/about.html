About Des

Hey there! I'm Des, a certified UX & UI Designer from Dublin, Ireland. I've always had a passion and interest in design, completing a BSc in Multimedia Computing initially in Swansea, Wales. My career path then focused on customer analytics, where I worked for some of the world's largest multinational companies using data to understand customer behaviours and communicating my findings using different visual tools.

UX appeals to me on many levels. It combines my interest in understanding people with my problem solving skills, curious nature and love for technology. UI allows me to explore my creative side and my visual design ability. Having knowledge in both areas allows me to add value to a company while also advocating for the user at all times. Creating products that positively impact on people's lives and the world around me.

I have a very informal and friendly manner and this helps me to work well in teams or when carrying out usability tests or user interviews. I'm always seeking out adventure! I enjoy travelling, nature, scuba diving, and keeping fit and healthy.
