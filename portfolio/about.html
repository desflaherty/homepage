About Des

I'm an experienced professional and have worked in a variety of customer centered roles to date. These roles all invloved understanding customer behaviours and needs and working with colleagues to solve complex business problems.

It is my love for technology as well as my desire to help others that lead me to the discipline of UX design.

My portfolio contains some of the products that I have created by following a user centered design approach, including primary research, analysis, ideation and prototyping.

I have a very informal and friendly manner and this helps me to work well in teams or when carrying out usability tests or interviews.

I'm always seeking out new places to go and new activities to try out! I enjoy travelling, nature, animals, martial arts, scuba diving, snowboarding and keeping fit and healthy.
