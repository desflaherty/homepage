Falcon Airlines

Graphic Design

The Brief:

From studying historic and contemporary design practitioners, the task was to complete a finished piece of work using one of the design techniques you have researched. The design should be based on images or locations that you encounter on your journey to work or that you would come in contact with on a daily basis.

Concept Development:

A comparative analysis previously undertaken focused on Swiss style graphic design and this was the style I developed for this project. My initial idea was to use the subject of flight as my topic choice. I researched Swiss style design pieces that could help formulate an idea for the piece. I reviewed posters created by Swiss designers such as Armin Hofmann, Max Huber and Joseph MÃ¼ller-Brockmann.

Development of A3 Poster using Adobe Illustrator:

The design uses negative space to create the image of a falcon. An airplane wing is used at the bottom part of the poster to give the poster some depth, and two identical shapes that represent an airline cockpit window to the right of the poster also create the tail appearance for the falcon image.
