DJ Black Coffee

The Brief

I chose to build a website for a well known DJ called 'Black Coffee'. He is a South African DJ, record producer and singer-songwriter. He has released 5 albums to date and won the "Breakthrough DJ of the Year" award at the DJ Awards in Ibiza in 2015. He has a large following on social media but does not have a dedicated website. The creation of a website would promote his music and would also act as a central hib for contacts where all media could be linked. The site would also increase his exposure on social media as well as providing a method to contact him or his management companies.

Concept Development

The site that was developed enables visitors to listen to 'Black Coffee's' latest album as well as providing a link to where they can purchase the album on amazon.co.uk. Visitors can also view a schedule of his upcoming tour dates and link to the respective website to purchase a ticket for these. The site provides links to his social media accounts on Facebook,Instagram and Twitter as well as YouTube videos. Visitors can also sign up to a mailing list and complete a booking request form. Management contact details are also provided.

UX

User stories were first developed to determine what the function of the website should be for a positive user experience. As a dance music fan or promoter I would want to:

  • Listen to a latest album so that I can then purchase it if I like the music.
  • Read about the DJ to become more interested in him as an artist.
  • View videos of his live performances so I can decide if I would like to go to his concert.
  • View his upcoming tour dates and link to a website where I can buy a ticket for a show.
  • View pictures of the artist to stay updated with his activity.
  • Make a booking request through the website to book the DJ.
  • View contact information for his management if I work in the press industry or wish to have a direct contact method.
