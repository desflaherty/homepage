Business Dashboard

Displaying KPI and transactional tables as well as a chart to track sales and revenue figures.

Overview

Collaborating with the business owner as well as the project manager, key metrics were proposed that could be tracked within the admin login view of the portal. These included revenue, orders, subscription counts and churn percentage. Tables were designed to display top 10 of products, revenue by country and by partner. The backend database team was heavily involved in conversations to determine what data was avaialble and data integrity issues were rasied. An early mockup for a potential design is shown below in this section. Conversations are ongoing as to how to progress with this user story dependent on other priorities within the team.
