Product Rebrand

In January 2023 SoftwareOne launched a rebrand initiative. As part of this the 'ONEClub' portal rebranded as the 'Partner Program'. A new colour scheme was introduced as directed by the global marketing team to align with the wider company product brand.

TL;DR

Meetings were initially held with the global marketing team where the rebrand was communciated. I worked with the front end development team to introduce the new look of the brand into the portal to align with the go live date as communicated. We adopted a phased approach firstly updating the name and logo for the portal and then updating the new brand colour scheme and the login and sign up pages for the portal.

Qualitative Research - Speaking To Our Partners

From partner preview calls it became evident that the current method of loading the full product catalogue was causing frustration for partners. At times they had to wait as the full catalogue was being loaded. Also the products being displayed to them were not personalised, they were being shown products that they had no interest in purchasing. They then had to use the product filters or the product search menu to find a particular product. In most cases partners purchased the same products repeatedly, so having to filter or search every time for a product was not the best experience and also took more effort and time for them.
