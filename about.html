About Des

Hey there!...I'm Des, a UX Product Designer from Dublin, Ireland. Before making a career change to product design I worked in marketing insights and sales analytics, as well as a number of CRM roles.

Now I'm using data, from customer interviews, UATs and analytics tools to improve the experience users have from software products that I work on.

In my role I combine my commercial experience with my constant desire to learn new skills and technologies, adding immediate value for a company while advocating for the user at all times.

I have a very friendly manner and this helps me to work well in multi-cultural teams or when carrying out usability tests or customer preview calls.

In my spare time I enjoy travelling, nature, scuba diving, and keeping fit and healthy.
