Customer Subscriptions

One of the most important job functions for the partners was to be able to easily navigate to their customer list so they could upsize their customer subscriptions. This improvement reduced the number of clicks needed to do this task.

TL;DR

From analysing the clicks against the main navigation cards on the homepage it became quickly evident that the 'View Customers' card was being used the most by partners. Observing this quantitative data lead me to investigate further during customer preview calls. I enquired about the main job functions that partners were doing when they were using the platform and they mentioned that they navigated to their customers so that they could upsize their customer subscriptions from the customer detail page. I investigated if we could reduce the number of clicks needed by bringing customer subscriptions up a level to the customer list - thus reducing the number of clicks needed. So this design improvement used quantitative and qualitative research to implement an improvement to the existing functionality, reducing the number of clicks needed to accomplish an important task for partners.

Quantitative Research - Homepage Navigation Cards

The 'View Customers' card on the homepage had the highest engagement out of the navigation cards shown here. Using 'Heap Analytics' clicks against each of the homepage cards was obtained. The 'View Customers' had 66% of the engagement across these cards in the last 30 days.

Qualitative Research - Speaking to our partners

I wanted to know more about the high engagement on the 'View Customers' card. The best way to understand why this card was being interacted with so much was to speak to partners. They informed me that one of their main job functions was to upsize their customer subscriptions.
