Updating the Funnel & the Shopping Cart Indicator

The introduction of Microsoft's New Commerce Experience lead to a change in the design of the product cards. The metrics tracking the shopping funnel had to be updated. This also lead to an update to the design of the shopping cart indicator.

TL;DR

Working closely with other members of the business that were involved with the portal I introduced a number of important announcements to inform partners of issues and changes to licensing as well as additional informative information on elements within the portal. I used a variety of components from the design library to introduce these business communicaitons and messages including tooltips, information banners, modals and visuals on the homepage.

Subscriptions List (Before Re-design)

The subscriptions list was used by partners to view the subscriptions they had bought on behalf of their customers. Just as SoftwareONE was a Microsoft reseller, partners that used the ONEClub partner portal also acted as resellers, purchasing licenses on behalf of their customers. The subscriptions list below shows the subscription status, product name, quantity, price, customer name and subscription end date. What was missing here was a way for partners to clearly see which subscriptions were on auto-renew, when subscriptions were set to renew and also which subscriptions were expiring soon or had already expired.

Subscriptions List (After Re-design)

The screenshot below shows a mockup design for proposed improvements to the subscriptions list page. There were a number of improvements including;

  • Badges to show whether the auto-renewal toggle switch in the subscriptions detail page was on or off.
  • New status indicators for the subscriptions to show if they are expiring soon or have expired already.
  • Badges to display the number of days left before the subscription renews or expires, to be displayed under the subscription end date.
