UX Improvements

Improving the experience for partners using the ONEClub portal. There were many improvements made to the partner portal based on customer feedback, business requirements, data anlaytics and UX reviews. Here I have documented some of the user stories that I worked on while also working on larger projects and numerous additional improvements to the platform.

Mini Cart

Re-designing the mini cart to introduce common design patterns and reduce cart abandonment.

Information Architecture

Improving accessibility by analysing the user interaction on the main navigation elements.

Customer Subscriptions

Reducing the number of clicks needed for partners to upsize their customer subscriptions.

Product Catalogue

Customising the product catalogue based on a partners previous purchase history.

Subscription Renewals

Updating the subscriptions list to inform partners of subscription renewals or expiry.

Administration Dashboard

Designing a dashboard that the PO and PM can use to track important business KPIs.
